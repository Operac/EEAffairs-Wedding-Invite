How It Began

A Thread That Chose Us

It all started with a random scroll through a friend’s WhatsApp status. She’s a jack-of-all-trades, always juggling a million things, and among the many pictures she posted that day was a photo of him. Out of pure amusement, Elizabeth replied, jokingly asking if matchmaking had been added to her long list of businesses. They laughed it off. She didn’t think much more of it.

Little did she know, fate was already at work.

A few days later, a message appeared in her TikTok DMs. It was him. Their mutual friend had quietly sent him her page, and he hadn’t hesitated.

At the time, Elizabeth was going through a heavy, draining season in her life and didn’t have much energy to give. But Ebenezer was consistent. He stayed steady, patient, and intentional, until her walls came down.

She’ll never forget one of the very first things he ever said to her:

“You’re my wife, and it’s you I’m getting married to. Even God has sealed it.”

Like he knew right from the very start. Today, those words are their reality. Elizabeth is marrying the man who truly sees her, loves and protects her fiercely, respects her opinions, encourages her dreams, and loves her so deeply.

Te amo, Teddy Bear.

FAQs

Questions?

Can I bring a plus-one?

Your access card admits one person only. Plus-ones are not permitted unless specifically noted on your invitation.

Are children allowed?

This is an adult event. Children are not permitted, with the exception of children belonging to close family. If you are unsure, please contact us before the day.

What time should I arrive?

Doors open at 1:00 PM. We strongly recommend arriving by 1:30 PM at the latest. The ceremony begins promptly at 2:00 PM and late arrivals may not be seated once proceedings have started.

Is there parking?

Yes, parking is available on-site at Jigiwura Event Centre.

What is the dress code?

Groomsmen and aso ebi ladies are in Mint Green. General invitees are in Lilac and Purple tones. See the Dress Code section for full details.

Can I take photos?

Please follow the photographer’s lead during the ceremony itself. At the reception, you are welcome to take photos and we’d love for you to share them via the QR code on your table.

Is it an outdoor event?

No. The celebration is fully indoors at Jigiwura Event Centre.

How do I get there?

The venue is at 153 Obafemi Awolowo Rd, Ikorodu, Lagos. Use the map and direction buttons in the Venue section above. If travelling from Lagos Island or the Mainland, allow at least 90 minutes on a Saturday.

How do I purchase Aso Ebi fabric?

When is the RSVP deadline?

October 31, 2026. Please do not leave it late.
